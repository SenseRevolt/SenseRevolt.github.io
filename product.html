About this book

Wrap the holiday season in kindness, movement, and music. "Christmas Kindness & Joy" is part of the My Kid's Big Feelings Matter series — a multisensory SEL activity book designed to help young children develop emotional vocabulary, empathy, and social skills through joyful holiday experiences.

Unlike traditional workbooks, each page invites your child to act, feel, draw, and move. QR codes unlock original songs like "Clap and Shine — It's Christmas Time!" that reinforce each lesson through rhythm and play.

A perfect Christmas gift for families, therapists, teachers, and school counselors.
