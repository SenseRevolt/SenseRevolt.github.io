Our Story

Born from the Storm.
Built for the Body
When Breathing Wasn't Enough.

SenseRevolt™ is a project born from necessity, created by two sisters from Ukraine. Living through profoundly turbulent times, we realized that standard wellness advice simply doesn't work when your nervous system is truly overloaded.

When the war started, Yani began hand-drawing intricate patterns to ground her nervous system and find a state of deep hyperfocus. It worked — even in circumstances where very little else did. So we decided to share what we learned with anyone searching for something that actually reaches the body, not just the mind.

We combined our personal struggles and professional skills to build a complete emotional ecosystem — real, working tools to protect mental peace for both adults and children. Because nervous system regulation isn't only about calming the storm. Sometimes it's about finding the signal again.
